TRIBUTE

ZAHRAA AL-TAYYAR

We met in architecture school at the University of Westminster. We were both in the same class and shared the same passion for designing and creating. We quickly became friends and started to spend a lot of time together, studying and working on projects. We bonded over our shared love for art and design and soon realized that we had a lot in common. We went on a holiday together to Greece and Italy, where we explored the ancient ruins and architecture of both countries.

Zahraa is an individual of munificent proclivities, a characteristic that is exemplified by her willingness to allot her time, resources, and support to those in her ambit. Furthermore, her predilection for adventure leads her to constantly seek out new experiences and locales, infusing her relationships with a sense of exhilaration and vitality. Additionally, her disposition is marked by an innate purity of heart.

Zahraa is person who is generous, adventurous, and pure-hearted and is a wonderful friend to have. She is willing to give of herself, her time, resources, or support, to help those around her. Her adventurous spirit leads her to try new things and explore new places, bringing excitement and energy to her relationships. She has a pure heart, a rare and valuable trait that means she is genuine, kind and compassionate. She has good intentions, easy to trust and confide in, which makes her the type of person that can foster deep connections and a sense of understanding.
